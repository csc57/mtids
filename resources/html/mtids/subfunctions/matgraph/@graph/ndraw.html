PURPOSE

ndraw(g) --- draw g in a figure window with numbered vertices

SYNOPSIS

function ndraw(g,dir,line_style,nodeFillColor,nodeEdgeColor)

DESCRIPTION 

 ndraw(g) --- draw g in a figure window with numbered vertices
 ndraw(g,dir) --- treat g as directed
 ndraw(g,dir,line_style) --- lines have given line_style
